### Why TikTok for Pulse Graphix?
- **Viral Potential:** One video can reach 100K-1M+ views organically

- **Age:** 18-35 (60% of French TikTok users)
- **Interests:** Design, entrepreneurship, tech, side hustles, personal branding
- **Behaviors:** Short attention span, prefer authentic over polished, value entertainment + education
- **Pain Points:** Want to start a business but don't know where to begin, need inspiration
- **Budget:** Lower than LinkedIn leads BUT growing (future high-value clients)

### TikTok Algorithm (2026)
1. **Watch Time:** Videos watched to the end get MASSIVE reach
2. **Completion Rate:** If 60%+ finish, TikTok shows it to 10x more people
3. **Hook = Everything:** First 1-2 seconds determine if people scroll past
4. **Re-watches:** If people watch twice, algorithm LOVES it
5. **Shares > Likes:** Sharing = strong signal of value
6. **Trending Audio:** Using popular sounds = 3-5x reach boost
7. **Niche Down:** Algorithm pushes content to specific communities (find yours)

**Key Insight:** TikTok rewards RETENTION more than any other platform. Every second matters.

### Equipment Needed
**Minimum:**
- ‚úÖ Smartphone (iPhone 11+ or Android equivalent)
- ‚úÖ Natural light or ring light (‚Ç¨30-50)
- ‚úÖ Tripod or phone holder (‚Ç¨20-30)

**Pro Setup (optional):**

### Editing Apps
**Primary: CapCut (free, powerful)**
- Templates, effects, auto-captions
- Trending sounds library
- Speed ramping, transitions
- Text animations

**Alternatives:**
- InShot (mobile, easy to use)
- Adobe Premiere Rush (more advanced)
- VN Video Editor (clean, professional)

### Video Structure (The "Hook-Retain-CTA" Formula)

**Seconds 0-3: THE HOOK**
- First frame must STOP the scroll
- Use bold text, movement, or shocking visual
- Ask a question or make a bold claim
- Examples:
  - "This website cost ‚Ç¨20,000 üëá"
  - "POV: You just hired a bad web designer"
  - "I'm about to expose the web design industry üò¨"

**Seconds 3-25: THE VALUE**
- Deliver on the hook's promise
- Show the process, transformation, or tips
- Keep it fast-paced (cut every 2-3 seconds)
- Use text overlays to reinforce message
- Add trending music/sound

**Seconds 25-30: THE CTA**
- "Follow for daily design tips"
- "Comment 'GUIDE' for the full checklist"
- "Share this with a business owner who needs to see it"
- "Part 2 coming tomorrow (make sure you're following)"

### Technical Specifications
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical, full-screen)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 minimum (4K preferred)
- **Length:** 15-60 seconds ideal (can go up to 10 min, but shorter = better)
- **File Size:** Under 287.6 MB
- **Captions:** ALWAYS include (70% watch muted)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant (not spammy)

## üí° ADVANCED TACTICS

### Tactic 1: The "Hook Test" Method
**Problem:** You don't know which hook will perform best
**Solution:** Post the SAME video with 3 different hooks (different days)

**Example:**
- Video: Before/after website redesign
- Hook A: "This website cost ‚Ç¨20,000 üëá"
- Hook B: "POV: You hire a professional designer"
- Hook C: "Watch me redesign this site in 60 seconds"

Post all three over one week. Winner gets scaled (repost with slight variations).

### Tactic 2: Comment-to-Content Pipeline
**How it works:**
1. Someone asks a question in comments
2. Reply: "Great question! Making a video on this tomorrow. Make sure you're following!"
3. Create video answering that question
4. Tag the person who asked in caption
5. They share = free promotion

**Benefit:** Endless content ideas + guaranteed engagement (person who asked + their network).

### Tactic 3: The "Series" Strategy
**Format:** Multi-part videos that create binge-watching

**Examples:**
- "Redesigning bad websites: Day 1/30"
- "I'm designing a website live: Part 1"
- "Teaching my [friend/family] design: Episode 1"

**Why it works:**
- People follow to see next part
- Algorithm recognizes series, pushes all parts
- Builds anticipation

**Pro Tip:** Film all parts in advance, post consistently (daily or every other day).

### Tactic 4: Duet Challenges
**Create a challenge for your audience:**

**Example:**

## üö® COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Mistake 1: Posting Inconsistently
**Problem:** Post 7 videos one week, then nothing for 2 weeks
**Solution:** Batch film content, schedule in advance, maintain rhythm

### Mistake 2: Ignoring Trends
**Problem:** Only post what YOU want, ignore what's trending
**Solution:** 70% your content, 30% trending/viral attempts

### Mistake 3: No Hook
**Problem:** Video starts with "Hey guys, today I'm going to..."
**Solution:** Start with the value. "This trick saved me 10 hours üëá"

### Mistake 4: Too Polished
**Problem:** Overproduced, looks like an ad
**Solution:** TikTok favors authentic. Phone camera, natural light = enough.

### Mistake 5: Not Responding to Comments
**Problem:** Post and ghost
**Solution:** Reply to every comment in first 2-4 hours (boosts algorithm)

### Mistake 6: Forgetting Captions
**Problem:** No text overlays or subtitles
**Solution:** 70% watch muted. Add captions to EVERY video.

### Mistake 7: External Links Too Soon
**Problem:** "Link in bio" when you have 50 followers
**Solution:** Build audience first (500+), then monetize.

### Mistake 8: Giving Up Too Early
**Problem:** Post 10 videos, get 200 views each, quit
**Solution:** Viral growth is exponential. Video #47 might be your breakout.
