## üìä Content Production Tips

### Batching Strategy:
- **Film 4-6 TikToks in one session** (different outfits/backgrounds)
- **Write 10 Twitter threads in one brainstorm** (schedule throughout month)
- **Design 5 LinkedIn carousels at once** (use template, swap content)
- **Record 2-3 YouTube videos per filming day** (different topics)

### Repurposing Framework:
1. **Start with YouTube** (long-form, detailed)
2. **Extract clips** ‚Üí TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts
3. **Write summary** ‚Üí LinkedIn article + carousel
4. **Pull quotes** ‚Üí Twitter thread
5. **Localize** ‚Üí Facebook post for community
6. **Archive** ‚Üí Blog post on website (SEO)

One piece of content ‚Üí 10+ posts across platforms

### Easy vs. Hard Days:
- **Low-energy days:** Text posts, polls, reposts, engagement
- **High-energy days:** Filming, complex tutorials, detailed carousels
- **Medium-energy:** Writing threads, designing simple graphics, editing

### Tools for Efficiency:
- **Canva:** LinkedIn carousel templates
- **CapCut:** TikTok editing
- **Buffer/Later:** Scheduling
- **Notion:** Content calendar
- **Obsidian:** Idea capture
