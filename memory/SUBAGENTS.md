## SPAWNING RULES

### When to Spawn Sub-Agent
1. **Complex task** â€” More than 5 steps
2. **Long-running** â€” Would take >10 minutes
3. **Parallel work** â€” Multiple independent tasks
4. **Specialized** â€” Requires focused expertise
5. **Background** â€” Can run while I do other things

### When NOT to Spawn
1. **Quick questions** â€” I answer directly
2. **Simple lookups** â€” I handle it
