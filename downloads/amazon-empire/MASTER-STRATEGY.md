This is the money machine. Here's exactly how it works:

### The Flow

```
FREE BOOK (Lead Magnet)
    ↓
BookFunnel Landing Page
(reader enters email to download)
    ↓
MailerLite captures email
    ↓
Automated Welcome Sequence (5 emails over 14 days)
    ↓
Reader is now on our LIST
    ↓
Launch emails when new books drop
    ↓
SALES + Amazon algorithm boost
    ↓
Amazon promotes our book to MORE readers
    ↓
REPEAT 🔄
```

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Day 0 | Book delivery + warm thank you | Deliver the goods, first impression |

| Day 5 | Recommend Book 1 (paid) + reader survey | First soft sell + learn about reader |
| Day 9 | Behind the scenes + exclusive content | Deepen relationship |
| Day 14 | "Here's everything I've written" | Full catalog pitch |

- New release announcements
- Exclusive previews/chapters
- Reader polls (what should I write next?)

4. **Social Media** — Instagram/TikTok for BookTok/Bookstagram audience
5. **Amazon "Also By" page** — every book links to your Author Central page
