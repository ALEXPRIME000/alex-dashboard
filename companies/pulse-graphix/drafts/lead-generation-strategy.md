## Target Market Analysis

### Primary Target: Small to Medium Businesses in Paris/Île-de-France

**Industries with High Web Design Needs:**

1. **Restaurants & Cafés**
   - Many still have outdated or no websites
   - Need: Menu display, reservation system, photos
   - Budget: 800€ - 2,500€
   - Approach: Walk-in visits, Google Maps research

2. **Retail Boutiques**
   - Fashion, jewelry, home decor shops
   - Need: E-commerce, catalog, brand identity
   - Budget: 1,500€ - 5,000€
   - Approach: Instagram research, local shopping areas

3. **Professional Services**
   - Lawyers, accountants, consultants
   - Need: Professional website, SEO, credibility
   - Budget: 1,500€ - 3,500€
   - Approach: LinkedIn, professional directories

4. **Healthcare Practitioners**
   - Doctors, dentists, physiotherapists
   - Need: Booking system, professional image
   - Budget: 1,500€ - 4,000€
   - Approach: Doctolib listings, medical directories

5. **Real Estate Agencies**
   - Need: Property listings, virtual tours
   - Budget: 3,000€ - 10,000€
   - Approach: SeLoger, local agencies

6. **Construction & Tradespeople**
   - Plumbers, electricians, builders
   - Need: Portfolio, contact, SEO local
   - Budget: 800€ - 2,000€
   - Approach: Pages Jaunes, local ads

7. **Event Planners & Wedding Services**
   - Need: Portfolio, booking, testimonials
   - Budget: 1,500€ - 4,000€
   - Approach: Wedding directories, Instagram

8. **Fitness & Wellness**
   - Gyms, yoga studios, coaches
   - Need: Class schedules, booking, community
   - Budget: 1,000€ - 3,000€
   - Approach: Instagram, local fitness forums

## Lead Generation Channels

### 1. LinkedIn Outreach
**Action:** Search for business owners in Paris who post about starting/growing their business

**Search filters:**
- Location: Paris, France
- Title: Founder, CEO, Owner, Gérant
- Industry: Retail, Food, Professional Services
- Recent activity: New business announcements

**Template:** Use email template #1 (cold outreach)

### 2. Google Maps Research
**Action:** Search for businesses with poor/no websites

**Method:**
1. Search "restaurant torcy" or similar
2. Check if website exists
3. If poor/none, note contact info
4. Send personalized outreach

### 3. Local Business Events
**Where to find:**
- Meetup.com - entrepreneur groups
- CCI Paris Île-de-France events
- BNI networking groups
- Startup events (Station F, etc.)

### 4. Referral Program
**Offer to existing clients:**
- 10% commission for referrals
- Or 100€ discount on next project

### 5. Social Media Content
**LinkedIn:**
- Post 3x/week about web design tips
- Share before/after projects
- Engage with local business content

**Instagram:**
- Portfolio showcase
- Design process behind-the-scenes
- Client testimonials

### 6. Local SEO
**Optimize for:**
- "Création site web Paris"
- "Agence web Torcy"
- "Web designer Île-de-France"
