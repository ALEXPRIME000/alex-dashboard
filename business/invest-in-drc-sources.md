## 8. US-DRC Critical Minerals Agreement (2025)

### Background

In **December 2025**, the DRC, the United States, and Rwanda signed three agreements described as a milestone for peace and economic integration. Key elements:

- **US-DRC Minerals Cooperation Framework**: The DRC offered a shortlist of strategic mining assets to the United States, including the **Rubaya coltan mine** (one of the world's richest tantalum deposits, currently under M23/rebel control in Nord-Kivu).
- **Glencore Stake Acquisition**: A preliminary commercial agreement was reached for US-backed interests to acquire a **40% stake in Mutanda Mining and Kamoto Copper Company** (Glencore's two largest DRC operations).
- **US Government Mining Vehicle**: Reports indicate the US government plans to establish a **new mining entity** to operate in Africa's Copperbelt (DRC and Zambia) to secure critical mineral supply chains.
