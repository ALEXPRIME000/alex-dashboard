## 1. OFFICIAL GOVERNMENT SOURCES

### 1.1 Ministère des Mines (Ministry of Mines)
- **URL:** https://mines.gouv.cd/
- **Provides:** Mining legislation, geological maps, mining rights administration, regulatory updates
- **Why:** Primary government authority for all mining sector regulation in DRC
- **Contact:** Web contact form; webmail at https://webmail.mines.gouv.cd/
- **Integration:** No public API; monitor press releases manually

### 1.2 CAMI — Cadastre Minier (Mining Registry)
- **URL:** https://camigrandkatanga.org/
- **Provides:** Mining rights registration, permit applications, cadastral maps, title registry
- **Why:** Official repository of ALL mining rights and titles in DRC
- **Contact:** Physical access for most records; some online queries
- **Integration:** Limited digital; direct relationship recommended

### 1.3 ANAPI — Agence Nationale pour la Promotion des Investissements
- **URL:** https://www.anapi.cd/
- **Provides:** Investment facilitation, one-stop business registration, investment opportunities database, tax incentives info
- **Why:** Primary government agency for foreign direct investment promotion
- **Contact:** info@anapi.cd — Kinshasa headquarters
- **Integration:** Key partner for investor onboarding; link prominently

### 1.4 Banque Centrale du Congo (BCC)
- **URL:** https://www.bcc.cd/
- **Provides:** Exchange rates, monetary policy, banking supervision, economic statistics
- **Why:** Central bank — sole authority on monetary statistics and exchange rate policy
- **Contact:** info@bcc.cd — 563, Blvd. Colonel Tshiatshi, Gombe, Kinshasa
- **Integration:** No public API; rates need manual curation

### 1.5 Ministère de l'Économie Nationale
- **Provides:** Trade policy, economic development programs, business environment reforms
- **Why:** Sets overall economic policy framework for investment

### 1.6 DRC Embassy in USA
- **URL:** https://www.ambardcusa.org/
- **Provides:** Investment info for US investors, sector guides, consular services
- **Contact:** Washington DC office
- **Integration:** Useful for US investor outreach content

### 1.7 Presidency / Primature
- **URL:** https://www.presidence.cd/
- **Provides:** Presidential decrees, high-level policy announcements, bilateral agreements
- **Why:** Source for major policy shifts affecting investment climate

## 2. MINING & MINERALS AUTHORITIES

### 2.1 US-DRC Critical Minerals Agreement (2025)
- **URL:** https://www.state.gov/ (search: DRC critical minerals partnership)
- **Provides:** Official bilateral agreement text, framework for US investment in DRC mining, critical minerals supply chain coordination
- **Why:** Highest-level bilateral commitment — THE landmark deal driving current DRC investment interest
- **Contact:** US State Department Bureau of African Affairs
- **Integration:** Embed official agreement link prominently; major credibility marker

### 2.2 USGS — U.S. Geological Survey
- **URL:** https://www.usgs.gov/centers/national-minerals-information-center
- **Provides:** Mineral commodity summaries, DRC-specific mineral statistics, cobalt/copper/lithium/coltan production data
- **Why:** Global gold standard for mineral statistics; scientifically rigorous, public domain data
- **Contact:** Public data
- **Integration:** **API available** at https://minerals.usgs.gov/ — ideal for embedding mineral data widgets

### 2.3 British Geological Survey (BGS)
- **URL:** https://www.bgs.ac.uk/
- **Provides:** World Mineral Production reports, critical minerals lists, geological assessments
- **Why:** Respected independent geological authority with DRC coverage
- **Integration:** Annual reports downloadable; some data APIs

### 2.4 Cobalt Institute
- **URL:** https://www.cobaltinstitute.org/
- **Provides:** Cobalt market data, responsible sourcing standards, supply chain insights
- **Why:** THE authority on cobalt — DRC produces ~70% of world cobalt
- **Integration:** Market reports, responsible sourcing content

### 2.5 London Metal Exchange (LME)
- **URL:** https://www.lme.com/
- **Provides:** Real-time and historical prices for cobalt, copper, and other metals
- **Why:** Global benchmark pricing authority
- **Integration:** **API available** (paid) for real-time price widgets

### 2.6 DRC Mining Week / VUKA Group
- **URL:** https://wearevuka.com/mining/drc-mining-week/
- **Provides:** Annual mining conference and expo, industry networking
- **Why:** Premier industry event for DRC mining sector
- **Contact:** +27 21 700 3500 — held annually in Lubumbashi
- **Integration:** Event calendar integration; sponsorship opportunities

## 3. INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS

### 3.1 World Bank — DRC
- **URL:** https://www.worldbank.org/en/country/drc/overview
- **Data Portal:** https://data.worldbank.org/country/CD
- **Provides:** Development projects, economic indicators, investment climate assessments, sector reports
- **Why:** Premier multilateral institution; rigorous data standards
- **Integration:** **FREE API** at `http://api.worldbank.org/v2/country/CD` — JSON format, no key required ✅

### 3.2 IMF — International Monetary Fund
- **URL:** https://www.imf.org/en/Countries/COD
- **Provides:** Article IV reports, debt sustainability analyses, economic outlook, policy recommendations
- **Why:** Primary international authority on DRC macroeconomic stability
- **Integration:** RSS feed for press releases; PDF reports linkable

### 3.3 African Development Bank (AfDB)
- **URL:** https://www.afdb.org/en/countries/central-africa/democratic-republic-of-congo
- **Provides:** Country strategy papers, infrastructure projects, economic sector work
- **Why:** Regional development bank with deep DRC expertise
- **Integration:** XML data feeds for registered users

### 3.4 EITI — Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative
- **URL:** https://eiti.org/countries/democratic-republic-of-congo
- **Provides:** Revenue transparency reports, mining sector governance data, payment disclosures
- **Why:** Global standard for transparency in extractive industries; DRC is a member
- **Integration:** **Open data** — downloadable datasets, embeddable charts

### 3.5 UNCTAD — UN Conference on Trade and Development
- **URL:** https://unctad.org/
- **Provides:** FDI statistics, investment policy reviews, bilateral investment treaties
- **Why:** UN authority on investment flows to developing countries
- **Integration:** Investment data API available

### 3.6 IFC — International Finance Corporation
- **URL:** https://www.ifc.org/
- **Provides:** Private sector investment data, project finance, business environment assessments
- **Why:** World Bank Group's private sector arm; active in DRC
- **Integration:** Project database searchable

### 3.7 UNDP — UN Development Programme
- **URL:** https://www.cd.undp.org/
- **Provides:** Human development indicators, governance programs, sustainability data
- **Why:** Broad development context for investment decisions

6. **Events Calendar** — DRC Mining Week, Mining Indaba, other conferences
7. **Investment Guides** — Curated from ANAPI, World Bank, IMF reports
