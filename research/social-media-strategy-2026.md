- **Fast pacing:** No filler, no boring sequences, rapid cuts
- **Rising tension:** Each segment more intense than the last
- **Meaningful payoff:** The ending delivers on the title's promise

4. **YouTube is psychology, not math** ‚Äî understand what humans want to click and watch
